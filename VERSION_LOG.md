## Rationale for Pivot

The pivot was driven by:

1. **Course alignment:** The course explicitly emphasizes *audience interaction analysis*, *trend tracking*, and *reproducible projects* for journalism/communication contexts. Our dashboard prototype demonstrates exactly that.

2. **Timeline feasibility:** With a 1.5-week sprint, we needed a focused, achievable scope. Pivoting to a methodological prototype allowed us to prioritize a clean, deployable pipeline over extensive experimentation.

3. **Team expertise:** Our team members' academic backgrounds in journalism and communication made the information-engagement framing a more natural fit.

The technical core—simulation, telemetry, modeling, dashboard—remains identical; only the framing and analytical questions have been refined. This demonstrates **methodological transferability** across domains.
